# France — Player Profiles

## Kylian Mbappé (FW)
- **Role**: Central striker / false 9. France's entire attack is built around his movement.
- **WC2026 stats**: 4G/12sh, G/SoT 0.57 (joint-highest in dataset), 6.03 shots/90, 0 yellow cards
- **Key insight**: Mbappé runs in behind. France's 11 through balls in 2 games are primarily designed to release him. He does not drop deep — he makes diagonal runs off the shoulder of the last defender. GKs face him at high speed with limited angles.
- **Discipline**: 0 yellow cards — no suspension risk

## Michael Olise (MF)
- **Role**: Right wing / attacking midfielder. France's best regular starter by On-Off.
- **WC2026 stats**: +2.87 On-Off — the best of any regular France starter
- **Key insight**: Olise creates the space that releases Mbappé. His movement pulls defenders wide, opening the through-ball lanes France exploit.

## Bradley Barcola (MF)
- **Role**: Left wing / impact substitute.
- **WC2026 stats**: +2.84 On-Off, G/SoT 1.00 — scored every shot on target
- **Key insight**: Elite impact sub. When France need a goal, Barcola enters and immediately converts. His G/SoT 1.00 is an outlier — small sample, but the pattern is real.

## Dayot Upamecano (DF)
- **Role**: Centre-back. Defensive anchor alongside Saliba.
- **WC2026 stats**: 6 tackles, 5 interceptions — leads France's squad in both
- **Key insight**: The Saliba-Upamecano pairing is arguably the best CB duo in the tournament. Upamecano covers ground, Saliba reads the game. Together they restrict opponents to 1 SoT/game.
