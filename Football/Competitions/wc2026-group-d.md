- Galarza (2') — early goal, Paraguay defended the lead the entire match.

### Matchday 3 (June 25 — pending)

## Key observations

- **USA**: Exceptionally efficient — 6 goals, 1 conceded, both conceded via own goals (no clean goals against). **Balogun leading the line effectively.** Home crowd advantage across all three matches.
- **Australia**: Solid defensively in MD1, but exposed by USA's pressure in MD2. Irankunda looks lively. Need to avoid defeat vs Paraguay to secure 2nd.
- **Paraguay**: Compact, counter-attacking. Won their must-win (vs Turkey) but were hammered by USA. Galarza crucial.
- **Türkiye**: **Worst team in the group** — 0 goals, 3 conceded, eliminated with a game to spare. No attacking output.
