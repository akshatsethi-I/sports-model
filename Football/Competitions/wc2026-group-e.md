## Match Results

### Matchday 1
**Germany 7–1 Curaçao** | June 14 | NRG Stadium, Houston
- Nmecha (6'), Schlotterbeck (38'), Havertz (45+5' pen, 88'), Musiala (47'), Brown (68'), Undav (78'); (Curaçao scorer not recorded)
- **Germany's biggest World Cup win in years.** Full attacking display. Musiala and Havertz electric. Undav off the bench, still scored.

**Côte d'Ivoire 1–0 Ecuador** | June 14 | Lincoln Financial Field, Philadelphia
- Diallo (90') — late winner, tight match throughout
- Ecuador dominated large portions but failed to convert. **Diallo's last-gasp goal completely changed the group dynamics.**

### Matchday 2
**Germany 2–1 Côte d'Ivoire** | June 20 | BMO Field, Toronto
- Undav (68', 90+4'); Kessié (30')
- Ivory Coast led for much of the match. **Undav the super-sub hero** — two goals after coming on. Germany won despite being second-best for long periods.

**Ecuador 0–0 Curaçao** | June 20 | Arrowhead Stadium, Kansas City
- Goalless draw leaves both teams on 1 point — MD3 is sudden death.
- Ecuador failed to score against the weakest team in the group. **Significant underperformance.**

### Matchday 3 (June 25 — pending)
- **Curaçao vs Côte d'Ivoire** | Lincoln Financial Field — Ivory Coast need a point; Curaçao need a win
- **Ecuador vs Germany** | MetLife Stadium — Germany can rotate; Ecuador need a win + Curaçao to not win

## Key observations

- **Germany**: **Outstanding — 9 goals in 2 games, +7 GD.** Havertz and Musiala the creative engine. Undav providing crucial impact from bench. Depth is elite. Could rotate heavily vs Ecuador.
- **Côte d'Ivoire**: Resilient but showed vulnerability to quality (conceded 2 late goals to Germany). Kessié crucial in midfield. Win over Ecuador via late goal suggests fortune is with them — but 2nd place not guaranteed.
- **Ecuador**: **The story of the group — failed to score against Curaçao.** Must win vs a potentially rotated Germany side and hope Curaçao don't beat Ivory Coast simultaneously. Very difficult position.
- **Curaçao**: Competitive (drew vs Ecuador) but outclassed by Germany. Need a result of a lifetime vs Ivory Coast.

## Key battle: MD3 Curaçao vs Côte d'Ivoire
This effectively decides 2nd place. If Ivory Coast win or draw, they go through. If Curaçao win, it depends on Ecuador vs Germany result. **Ivory Coast are heavy favourites.**
