# France

## Overview
WC2026. 5-game record: **W5 D0 L0**, 14 goals scored, 2 conceded. Advanced through group + R32 + R16 without dropping a point.

**Key caveat:** Goals vs xG: +3.35 over 5 games (10.65 xG, 14 goals). Overperformance has narrowed from +4.04 at 3G to +3.35 at 5G — regression is occurring but France's true quality is 2.1 xG/game, not 2.8 goals/game. Dembélé and Mbappé conversion rates remain above sustainable levels.

## Manager
Didier Deschamps. Structured, pragmatic. PPDA 12.6 (moderate press — improved from 14.3 at 3G). Primary mechanism: through balls (28 in 5G = 5.6/game) and fast transitions.

## Preferred Formation
4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1. Possession-based (60.5%, 88.3% pass%) with line-breaking through balls as the defining attacking pattern.

## Style of Play

**Build-up:** 60.5% possession (highest in tournament), 88.3% pass accuracy. 28 through balls (5.6/game). 978 final-third passes (195/game), 82.2% completion — elite final-third entry. NOT a crossing team (10.4% cross accuracy).

**Pressing:** PPDA 12.6 — moderate press. Press start 44.0m. 36 high turnovers (8 shot-ending, 2 goal-ending). More aggressive than earlier in the tournament.

**Attacking patterns:** Through-ball attacks primary. Fast breaks: 11 attempts, 3 goals (27%). Header goals: 1. SP goals: 1 (0.65 xG / 5G = 0.13/game).

**Defensive shape:** Allow only 1.8 SoT/game (best in dataset, 5G). Only 9 SoT conceded in 5 games. Opponents allowed only 12.8 touches in box/game (elite suppression). Aerial dominance: **64.7% aerial duels won**.

## Key Players

- **Kylian Mbappé** — FW, 7G/26sh/17SoT, G/SoT 0.41, SoT/90 3.49. Still scoring but conversion will regress.
- **Ousmane Dembélé** — MF/FW, 4G/12sh/5SoT, G/SoT 0.80 — overperforming. On-Off: +0.28.
- **Michael Olise** — MF/FW, 0G/14sh/5SoT, SoT/90 1.14. High volume, no conversion yet.
- **Bradley Barcola** — FW, 2G/6sh/3SoT. On-Off: +0.69.
- **Désiré Doué** — FW/MF, 1G/8sh/5SoT. On-Off: -0.43.
- **Malo Gusto** — DF. On-Off: +0.87 (was +5.06 at 3G — normalising).
- **Aurélien Tchouaméni** — MF. On-Off: +0.67. 1 YC only.
- **Mike Maignan** — GK. Save%: **77.8%** (7 saves / 9 SoTA, 5G). Major improvement from 66.7% at 3G. 1 penalty saved.

## Strengths
- Elite SoT suppression: 1.8/game allowed (best in dataset)
- Possession dominance: 60.5%, 978 FT passes/5G
- Aerial duels: 64.7% won (highest in QF bracket)
- Through-ball system: 5.6/game
- Box presence: 30.2 touches/game (highest in dataset)
- Maignan improving: 77.8% save% (5G)
- Discipline: 4 YC / 5G, 9.8 fouls/game

## Weaknesses
- Goals vs xG: +3.35 — overperformance will partially regress
- SP threat is modest: 0.13 xG/game from set pieces
- Opponent chances are low quality (xG/shot 0.11 allowed) but France has been fortunate too: xGA 3.68 conceded only 2 goals (-1.68 vs xG)
- Mbappé On-Off: -5.93 in 5G (structural drag persists)
