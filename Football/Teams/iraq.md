## Style of Play

**Defensively congested**: 16 interceptions, 22 tackles won — Iraq work hard without the ball. They block lanes and intercept. But when opponents break through (and they do — 10 SoT conceded in 2 games) the GKs can't save them.

**Cannot shoot on target**: 6.7% SoT% is structurally broken. Whether it's poor technique, rushed shooting under pressure, or attacking movement problems, Iraq essentially do not test GKs. Al Hamadi's 6 attempts from likely outside-box positions all missed the frame.

**Cross-heavy but ineffective**: 22 crosses in 2 games but no output. Al Ammari (6), Doski (4) deliver from wide without result.

**Being fouled frequently**: 21 times fouled in 2 games — set piece opportunities. Whether Iraq can take advantage of these depends on their dead-ball quality (pending Opta).
