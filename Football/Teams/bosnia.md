## Historical Lessons
- Bosnia's 5 goals in 3 games came on 1.87 xG. This is not sustainable. Their open-play threat is near-zero.
- Vasilj at 50% is a proven liability — opponents should model high conversion against him.
- SP (65% aerial duels won) is the only legitimate route to goal for Bosnia. Against teams with poor SP defence, Bosnia can score. Against teams with strong aerial defence, they are effectively toothless.
- 15.67 fouls/game creates constant card exposure — particularly dangerous for Katić and Dedić (both drag players already under discipline pressure).
