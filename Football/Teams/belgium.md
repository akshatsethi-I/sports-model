# Belgium

## Overview
WC2026. 5-game record: **W3 D2 L0**, 13 goals scored (Opta), 5 conceded. Advanced through group + R32 + R16.

**Key insight:** Belgium are the most prolific attacking team in the QF bracket (2.11 xG/game, 42.2 box touches/game — highest of all QF teams). However, their attacking output will be heavily suppressed by Spain's elite defensive structure (1.0 SoT allowed/game). Their critical defensive vulnerability is set pieces — 2 SP goals conceded from 15 SP shots (40% conversion rate against) in 5 games.

**Goals vs xG: +2.46** — significant overperformance. Regression risk.
