- **Key tactical battles:** Haaland aerial vs CIV's 0-header-goals-conceded defence. Nyland 40% save% vs CIV's 1.36 adj SoT — any CIV shot on target is genuinely dangerous.
- **Momentum triggers:** Norway early goal → CIV forced to open, Yan Diomandé more dangerous in space. CIV goal (via Kessié late run or Yan Diomandé transition) → Norway must chase, Ryerson's absence means less crossing quality.
