# Canada

## Overview
WC2026. **Qualified for Round of 32. R32 fixture: vs South Africa, Jun 28.**
Group record: 1W 1D 1L — but **+5 GD (8 goals scored, 3 conceded)** — dominant attacking output despite mixed results.

Yet only 3 goals conceded (7 SoTA, opponent G/SoT 0.43) — the defence restricts shots well but when they come through, Crépeau is vulnerable. 0 clean sheets despite allowing only 2.33 SoT/90. Opponents cross only 19 times against Canada across 3 games — Canada's wide defence holds.
