Alisson stops everything in close range.

**Transition play**: Vinicius Jr (4G/12sh, SoT% 66.7%) as the primary threat — pace-based, wide left, cuts inside. Matheus Cunha (3G, G/SoT 0.75) as an excellent secondary striker. Direct speed: 1.48 — fast transitions despite patient possession base.
